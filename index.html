Our Story

On the 12th of March 2025, in a simple group chat, two souls quietly crossed paths. There was no sign of what was to come—no dramatic beginning, no special spark. But Limon noticed her. And over time, through short texts, shared moments, and long silences, Tanisha began to mean something to him.

Their bond was fragile at first. Unsure. But something grew.

Limon didn't fall in love the usual way. There wasn't one moment where everything changed. It was quiet. Slow. He didn't think she was the most special girl in the world when they first met. In fact, he admitted to himself that she wasn't different from others at first. But somehow, she became important because of the way he saw her. He gave her meaning. And that made her special.
